Detailed Instructions

1 Creating Your First Audience

arrow_forward

Click "Create Audience"

Navigate to the Create Audience page from the main navigation.

arrow_forward

Start the AI Conversation

Begin chatting with our AI assistant. Be specific about your target audience demographics, interests, and behaviors.

arrow_forward

Answer AI Questions

The AI will ask follow-up questions to better understand your audience. Provide detailed answers for better results.

arrow_forward

Review Generated Keywords

Once the conversation is complete, review the AI-generated keywords and summary before proceeding.

2 Managing Your Audiences

arrow_forward

View All Audiences

Access the Manage Audiences page to see all your created audiences and their status.

arrow_forward

Monitor Search Progress

Track the progress of your audience searches in real-time. Large searches may take several minutes.

arrow_forward

View Audience Details

Click on any audience to view detailed information, conversation history, and search results.

3 Exporting Your Audience Data

arrow_forward

Wait for Search Completion

Ensure your audience search has completed successfully before attempting to export data.

arrow_forward

Download CSV File

Click the download button in the audience details page to get your audience data as a CSV file.

arrow_forward

Use in Marketing Campaigns

Import the CSV file into your preferred marketing platform or CRM system.
