play_circle Getting Started

What is Audience Dropper?

Audience Dropper is an AI-powered platform that helps you create highly targeted audiences for your marketing campaigns through intelligent conversation and advanced search capabilities.

How do I create my first audience?

Click "Create Audience" and chat with our AI to define your target audience. The AI will ask questions about your ideal customers and generate targeted keywords automatically.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can create up to 3 audiences for free to test our platform. No credit card required.

group_add Audience Creation

How does the AI audience creation work?

Our AI asks you questions about your target audience and uses advanced algorithms to generate relevant keywords and search criteria for finding your ideal customers.

Can I edit an audience after creation?

Currently, audiences cannot be edited after creation. You'll need to create a new audience with updated criteria if you want to make changes.

How long does audience creation take?

The AI conversation typically takes 5-10 minutes. The actual search process can take several minutes to hours depending on the size and complexity of your audience.

search Search & Results

How accurate are the search results?

Our search engine uses advanced algorithms and multiple data sources to provide highly accurate and relevant results for your target audience.

Why is my search taking so long?

Large audience searches can take time to ensure accuracy. You can monitor progress in real-time on the audience details page.

Can I export my audience data?

Yes! Once your search is complete, you can download the results as a CSV file for use in your marketing campaigns.

account_circle Account & Billing

How do I upgrade my account?

Go to your account settings and select a plan that fits your needs. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise accounts.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access to your account until the end of your billing period.
