Common Issues & Solutions

How do I create my first audience?

Use our AI-powered audience creation tool to define your target audience through a conversational interface.

Why is my audience search taking so long?

Large audience searches can take several minutes. You can monitor progress in the audience details page.

How do I export my audience data?

Once your audience search is complete, you can download the results as a CSV file from the audience details page.

Can I edit an audience after creation?

Currently, audiences cannot be edited after creation. You'll need to create a new audience with updated criteria.
